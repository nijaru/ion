# DeerFlow Notes (2026-03-27)

## Answer

DeerFlow is useful as a source of packaging and product-surface ideas, not as a direct architecture template for ion.

What transfers:
- clear separation between API-backed model providers and CLI-/agent-backed providers
- explicit skills surface
- explicit sandbox/security posture
- “one-line agent setup” and bootstrap docs aimed at other coding agents

What does not transfer:
- the heavier LangGraph/web-app/server architecture
- config-heavy multi-service orchestration
- a “super agent harness” scope that is broader than ion’s terminal-native product
